Kano

Kano is the leader of the organized crime syndicate known as the Black Dragon. Known as a scoundrel, a backstabber, and a thorn in the side of the Special Forces, Kano specializes in fighting dirty with his knives and his cybernetic enhancemnets to make him a nightmare to play againist if you are unaware of the dirty tricks he has up his sleeve.

In the Ripper variation, Kano gains access to new strings, a parry, and a command grab. His new strings include a launcher in his 241+3 string that will be your go to punish in this variation, the F43 to get extra damage when F4 hits and 221+3 where Kano can amplify the end of the string for increased damage. The parry(Rack Off) is especially helpful, and it can stop opponents who are playing offensively in their tracks. To be able to play this variation efficently, you need the use Kano's command grab (Lumbar Check) to its full ability. This command range has a bigger throw range than Kano's toward and back throws, and can be special canceled from his 11 and 24 string, and from D1, D3, or D4. All of these moves combined can make Kano a hassle to deal with.

Tips:

Rack Off(Parry) has a lot of active frames, this can frustrate your opponent, and make them second guess themselves about attacking you. Use this to your advanatge to pressure them.

How to Play:

  • Kano gains a mixup with his 11, 24 strings where you can special cancel into the command grab, or fully commit to the rest of the string (112, 241+3). Your opponent will have to guess if you are going for the command grab, or fully commiting to the rest of the string. Both the strings and command grab are punishable, so keep your opponent guessing by mixing up the command grab with the full strings.

  • Kano also gains a mixup with his D1,D3,and D4 buttons. You can either special cancel these buttons into the command grab(Lumbar Check), or the Kano Ball(BF3) Both of these mixups are very unsafe, so use these mixups carefully.
