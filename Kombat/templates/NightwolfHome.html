Deadly Talon(312):Triggers if only the final move HITS(2)

Rhino Charge Amplified (BF4AMP): Triggers when only the AMPLIFIED version HITS a GROUNDED OPPONENT

How to Play:

Nightwolf gains a launcher in his Rising Tomahawk move, and a damage buff with his Spirit of Kiba move. To be able to uses this variation properly, you need to be able to confirm into the Rising Tomahawk when you hit your opponent with a string. Make sure that your opponent is not blocking your string, as canceling a string into the Rising Tomahawk is very punishable. Use your damage buff sparingly or when you know you are in a safe position to do so. It is a buff to your damage, but opponents can easily punish you for activating it.
