What is Mortal Kombat 11?

Mortal Kombat 11 is the eleventh entry in the "Mortal Kombat' franchise, where fighters battle it out in gruesome and bloody fighters to acheive victory. Releasing in April of 2019, MK11 is a slow-paced strategic fighting game where every move you make in a tenth of a second can mean if you win a game, or lose. Including the lovable characters that made the series famous, MK11 has become a highly-competitive game where real players battle it out to earn fame and fortune. To get to the top, a player needs to know everything about not just how to play their preferred character, but how to play neutral, what each character's strength and weakness is, and most importantly, the frame data for each character, especially their own.

 

What is a fighting game?

The definition of a "fighting game" from Wikipedia: A fighting game is a video game genre based around close combat between a limited number of characters, in a stage in which the boundaries are fixed. The characters fight each other until they defeat their opponents or the time expires. The matches typically consist of several rounds, in an arena, with each player character having different abilities but each is relatively viable to choose. Players must master techniques such as blocking, punishing opponent mistakes, counter-attacking, and chaining attacks together into "combos" to acheive victiory.
